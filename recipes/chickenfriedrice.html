Chicken Fried Rice

Skill Level: 1

Skills:

  • Frying
  • Chopping Vegetables

Primary Ingredients

Medium/short-grain rice 32 oz
Chicken breast 4 oz
Eggs 2

Secondary Ingredients

Carrots To taste
Onions To taste
Scallions To taste
Minced garlic 1 tbspn
Unsalted butter 1 tbspn
Scallop soup stock To taste

Powders, Sauces, Oils

Vegetable oil To taste
Dashi 2 pinches
Chicken base 1 pinch
Black pepper 1 pinch
Salt 2-3 pinches
Soy sauce 1/2 oz
Sesame oil 1/2 oz

Video Demonstration

Instructions

  1. Cook rice
  2. Fry the eggs
  3. Add chicken
  4. Add the vegetables
  5. Add rice, break into clumps
  6. Add salt and pepper
  7. Add chicken base, garlic, and dashi
  8. Add butter and green onions
  9. Add soy sauce and sesame oil
