Chicken Stirfry

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken thigh
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup broccoli
  • 1 chopped carrot
  • 8 oz mushroom
  • 4 minced garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 1/2 chopped onion

Steps

  1. Cut chicken and vegetables
  2. Cook chicken until browned
  3. Add oil and butter to skillet with vegetables
  4. Stir all ingredients together
